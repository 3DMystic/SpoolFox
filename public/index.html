About SpoolFox

Anybody who 3D prints, knows the fatigue of searching for good filament to feed through their printers. The jumping from tab to tab on your browser to check prices and reviews, and compare one spool to another. No longer is this a needed with SpoolFox, as this page will display spools of filament from top brands with the best reviews and display the stats that you need to find the right match for you and your printers. The spools displayed are collected from various sites and will connect you directly to where you can purchase them on those sites, saving you time to searching for filament, to have more time to work on that next exciting project.
